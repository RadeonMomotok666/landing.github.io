New Customer?

To become a customer of OmniStar and get a permit with us - download and fill out the set up form for the new companies. After that, send it to permits@go-omni-star.com. You need also attach the copy of a document, or just put down your signature on an original document. Our manager will contact you shortly after receiving your form.

Already a Member?

If you're already our customer, download the permit application form, fill it out and send us back on permits@go-omni-star.com . You need also attach the copy of a document, or just put down your signature on an original document. Our manager will contact you shortly after receiving your form.
